High nitrate concentrations in drinking water are a health hazard. Recently a possible cancer risk in adults from nitrate (and nitrite) has emerged, but the magnitude of the risk is unknown. This application is designed to analyze nitrate from groundwater wells and cancer rate distribution in Wisconsin. Click the menu button on the left to enter variables to calculate linear regression. You can also toggle layers through the layer control button on the top right.
